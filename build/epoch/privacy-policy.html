Privacy Policy for Epoch

Introduction

Welcome to Epoch. This privacy policy outlines how Epoch, developed by Pavel Kroupa, handles your information. Epoch is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information Collection

Epoch does not collect any personal information from its users. The app functions independently without storing or accessing any user data.

Third-Party Services

Epoch offers the option to connect to Trakt via OAuth 2.0. When you choose to connect to Trakt, the app retrieves information from your Trakt account solely for the purpose of providing enhanced app functionality. This information is not shared, or used for any other purpose.

Trakt Integration

When you connect Epoch to your Trakt account, you will be subject to Trakt's privacy policy. Please review Trakt's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data: Trakt Privacy Policy.

Data Sources

Epoch uses data provided by The Movie Database (TMDB) and Trakt. All data is retrieved through HTTPS connections to ensure the security and privacy of the data during transmission.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the practices of Epoch, please contact Pavel Kroupa at pavel@withpavel.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Epoch may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Effective Date

This privacy policy is effective as of 2024-06-16.

By using Epoch, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
